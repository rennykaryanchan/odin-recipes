Kimchi Jjigae Recipe

Kimchi Jjigae is a popular Korean dish that is enjoyed in every Korean kitchen. It's hearty, delicous and very easy to make

Ingredients

  • 1 pound kimchi, cut into bite sized pieces
  • 1/4 cup of kimchi brine
  • 1/2 pound of pork shoulder
  • 1/2 package of tofu, sliced into 1/2 inch thick bite sized pieces
  • 3 green onions
  • 1 medium online, slices (1 cup)
  • 1 teaspoon of kosher salt
  • 2 teaspoons of sugar
  • 2 teaspoons of gochugaru
  • 1 tablespoon of gochujang
  • 1 teaspoon of toasted sesame oil
  • 2 cups of anchovy stock

Steps

  1. Place the kimchi and kimchi brine in a shallow pot, layer the pork and onion around the kimchi
  2. Slice 2 green onions (I like to do them diagonally for style points) and add them to the pot
  3. Add salt, sugar, hotpepper flakes, and hot pepper past. Drizzle the sesame oil over all of thism then add the anchovy stock
  4. Cover and cook for 10 minutes over medium high heat
  5. Open and mix in the seasonings with a spoon. Lay the tofu over the top
  6. Cover and cook for another 10 to 15 minutes over medium heat
  7. Chop 1 green onion and put it on top of the stew. Remove from heat and serve immediately with rice
