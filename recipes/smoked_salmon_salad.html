Kimchi Jjigae Recipe

Smoked salmon salads are a great way to make a healthy salad without using dressing!

Ingredients

  • 3 cups mixed greens
  • 1/2 red onion sliced
  • 1/2 cucumber chopped
  • 1 chopped avocado
  • 100g thinly sliced smoked salmon
  • 50ml lemon juice

Steps

  1. Place the greens in a large bowl.
  2. Top with cucumbers and sliced salmon.
  3. Top the rest of the salad with the rest of the ingredients.
  4. Drizzle the lemon juice over top of the salasd.
